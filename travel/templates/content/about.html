What is Travel Blog Source?

Improve search, growth and popularity of travel blogs

The purpose of travel blog source is to improve search, growth and popularity of travel blogs. Due to a lack of searchability and the sheer growth of travel blogs, in recent years it has been become difficult for new travel blogs to gain new readers.

How will Travel Blog Source accomplish this?

Many times readers are searching for information on a specific country or continent. The problem is that when using google, they receive the top 3 blogs which have been around for years. TBS allows readers to search for blogs in a country or region and sort by ratings and/or search the information provided by the blog owner.

There are also other options for blogs who are wanting to go the extra mile to build their customer base such as contributing blog articles that will be displayed on TBS, submitting banner pictures, or participating in social media.

If you are a travel blog owner, there is nothing to lose. Just fill out the short registration form and you will show up in search results! It only takes a minute to improve the world of travel blogging.
