Uploads

There are currently two types of uploads to help boost traffic to your site.

The first type of upload is Banner Picture which appears at the top of region pages, or the home page. Some regions are free, and some you will need to pay a small amount.

The second type of upload is a Blog Entry. You may upload a blog entry which is pertinent to a specific region. Some regions are free, and some you will need to pay a small amount.

But what does it do for me?

We are keeping a close eye on what Sponsorships can do for Travel Blogs. We're just as concerned as you are about improving the world of travel blogging.

There are a series of reports and charts to help understand how sponsorships affect traffic, and we will share them when there is enough data.
